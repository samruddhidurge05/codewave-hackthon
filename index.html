INTRODUCTION

In today's digital age, cyberattacks aren't just targeting machines — they're targeting people. From phishing scams to identity theft, individuals are becoming the front line of defense. Our goal is to turn awareness into action by creating immersive experiences that teach users how to spot, avoid, and respond to digital threats.

Whether you're a student, a working professional, or simply someone who uses the internet every day, understanding cybersecurity has never been more important. We're here to make that journey engaging, hands-on, and accessible.
